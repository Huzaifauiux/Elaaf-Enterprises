who we are?

Elaaf enterprises in Sialkot from Pakistan, that has been manufacturing high quality

Professional Sports Wears, active Wears, Casual Wear, street wear, outer wear,  

ym wear, patches and all kinds of sports Wear equipment. Our products are shipped

and delivered all around the World to countless satisfied Athletes that use our gear. Our

specialty is the ability to manufacture and ship our huge range of products at such

lucrative prices, as well as ensuring our customer’s satisfaction 100 % at all times. Our

product speaks for themselves and our consistency in delivery and quality is unmatched.

our core values

Our vision was to create more than just a place where people could buy affordable

sportswear in Pakistan. We wanted our customers to be able to be true to their style,

while maintaining tactile functionality. We have tried to maximize comfort by designing our

own unique measurement and fittings.

why choose us?

We are devoting all our passion and interests in presenting you the best quality custom

Sportswear globally at your desired Prices.

our process

fabric selection

Your dedicated

representative will

initially help you select

the best fabric for your

product. We have a

custom fabric

development facility as

low as 300 KGs per color.

Customization

At this step, all the

changes you need in terms

of printing, add-ons and

changes are done

effectively.

Cut & Sewing

Once the fabric is

selected, it is sent for Cut

and sew processes to

make sure that the

quality of the product is

not compromised at all.

Labeling

Once your product is

finalized, we make sure

that every piece has your

label before it is sent to

the packaging department.

Packaging

The final product is ironed,

folded with care, and

packed in the baggage of

your choice to give it a

premium look.

Shipment

Once the order is packed

and ready, it is sent for

shipment to your location

with minimum shipping time.
